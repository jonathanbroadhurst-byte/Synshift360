# Survey Participant Guide

## Welcome!

Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback through our 360-degree leadership assessment platform. Your honest, thoughtful feedback helps leaders grow and develop. This guide will walk you through the process step by step.

---

## Getting Started

### How You'll Receive Your Survey

You'll receive a survey invitation in one of these ways:

1. **Email invitation** with a direct link to the survey
2. **Survey code** (6 uppercase letters/numbers like "ABC123")
3. **Direct survey link** shared by the leader

### Accessing Your Survey

**Option 1: Using a Direct Link**
- Click the link provided in your email or message
- You'll go straight to the survey

**Option 2: Using a Survey Code**
1. Visit the survey access page
2. Enter the 6-character code you received
3. Click "Access Survey"

---

## What to Expect

### Survey Types

There are two types of surveys on this platform:

#### **SyncShift Personal** (Blue theme)
- **29 questions total**
- 26 rating questions (1-7 scale)
- 3 open-text questions
- Focuses on 7 leadership competencies
- Takes approximately 10-15 minutes

#### **Quantum Leadership Calibration 360** (Orange/Pink theme)
- **30 questions total**
- 30 rating questions (1-10 scale)
- Focuses on 10 core leadership competencies
- Takes approximately 12-18 minutes

### Survey Structure

Both surveys are organized by **competency categories** such as:
- Leadership & Direction
- Trust Intelligence
- Systems Awareness
- Emotional Regulation
- And more...

Each category label appears above the related questions to help you understand what aspect of leadership you're evaluating.

## Completing the Survey

### Rating Questions

**Understanding the Scale:**

For **SyncShift Personal** (1-7 scale):
- **1** = Strongly Disagree
- **4** = Neutral
- **7** = Strongly Agree

For **Quantum 360** (1-10 scale):
- **1** = Strongly Disagree
- **5-6** = Neutral
- **10** = Strongly Agree

**How to Rate:**
1. Read each question carefully
2. Think about specific examples or behaviors you've observed
3. Click the number that best reflects your assessment
4. You can change your answer anytime before submitting

**Tips for Rating:**
- Base your ratings on actual observations, not assumptions
- Consider multiple situations, not just one incident
- Be honest - this feedback is meant to help the leader improve
- Use the full range of the scale (don't just pick middle numbers)

### Open Text Questions

Some surveys include text questions asking for qualitative feedback, such as:
- "What are this leader's greatest strengths?"
- "What small shifts could create better alignment?"

**Tips for Text Responses:**
- Be specific and give examples when possible
- Focus on behaviors, not personality traits
- Balance constructive feedback with positive observations
- Keep responses professional and respectful
- Aim for 2-5 sentences per question

## Privacy & Anonymity

### Your Responses Are Completely Anonymous

- **No individual responses are ever shown** to the leader
- We use **encryption and GDPR-compliant security** measures
- Your responses **cannot be traced back to you**
- Only aggregated results are included in reports
- The system does not collect identifying information about participants

### How It Works

1. When you submit your feedback, it's stored with an encrypted participant identifier
2. Multiple responses are combined before being shown to the leader
3. Individual ratings and comments are never attributed to specific people
4. Leaders see trends, patterns, and aggregated scores - not individual responses

## Step-by-Step: Completing Your Survey

### Step 1: Access the Survey
- Use your survey code or click the direct link
- You'll see the leader's name and survey type

### Step 2: Review the Introduction
- Check how many questions to expect
- Note the rating scale being used
- Read the privacy notice

### Step 3: Answer Each Question
- Work through the questions in order
- Rate each question thoughtfully
- For text questions, provide constructive feedback
- You can scroll up and down to review your answers

### Step 4: Review Your Responses
- Scroll through to make sure you've answered all questions
- The "Submit Feedback" button will be **disabled** until you've answered at least one question
- Double-check any text responses for clarity

### Step 5: Submit
- Click the **"Submit Feedback"** button at the bottom
- You'll see a confirmation message
- Your responses are immediately recorded
- You **cannot edit responses after submission**

## Understanding the Rating Scale

### What Each Number Means

**For a question like: "Communicates a clear vision and direction"**

| Rating | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 1-2 | Rarely/Never | I almost never see this behavior |
| 3-4 | Sometimes | I see this occasionally, but not consistently |
| 5-6 | Often | This is generally true most of the time |
| 7-10 | Always/Consistently | This is consistently demonstrated |

### When to Use Each Part of the Scale

- **Low scores (1-3)**: This behavior is rarely or never demonstrated
- **Middle scores (4-6)**: This behavior is sometimes shown but inconsistently
- **High scores (7-10)**: This behavior is consistently and reliably demonstrated

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long will the survey take?
Most surveys take 10-18 minutes depending on the type. Take your time - quality feedback is more valuable than speed.

### Can I save and come back later?
No, surveys must be completed in one session. Make sure you have 15-20 minutes available before starting.

### Can I see my responses after submitting?
No, once submitted, responses cannot be viewed or edited. This protects anonymity.

### What if I don't know the answer to a question?
If you haven't observed a particular behavior or don't have enough information, you can:
- Use a neutral/middle rating
- Skip text questions if you don't have relevant feedback
- Rate based on your general impression

### Will the leader know who said what?
No. Individual responses are never shown. Only aggregated data and trends are included in the leader's report.

### What happens after I submit?
Your feedback is securely stored and will be combined with other participants' responses. When enough responses are collected, a comprehensive report is generated for the leader.

### Can I fill out multiple surveys?
Yes! You may be invited to provide feedback for different leaders. Each survey is separate and anonymous.

### What if I'm having technical problems?
- Make sure you're using a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact the person who sent you the survey for assistance

## Tips for Meaningful Feedback

### Think in Examples
Instead of: "They're good at communication"  
Think: "I've noticed they send clear weekly updates and make themselves available for questions"

### Be Specific
Instead of: "Needs to improve leadership"  
Think: "Could provide more context when delegating tasks so the team understands the bigger picture"

### Focus on Observable Behaviors
Instead of: "They seem stressed"  
Think: "Sometimes responds to questions quickly without fully listening to the concern"

### Balance Positive and Constructive
- Acknowledge genuine strengths
- Identify specific areas for growth
- Frame suggestions constructively
